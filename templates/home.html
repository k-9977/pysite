About the Palace Recruitment Hall

Nestled among drifting clouds and blooming lotus ponds, the Celestial Lotus Palace is a sanctuary beyond the bustle of the mortal world. Travelers, cultivators, scholars, musicians, and wandering souls alike are welcome to rest beneath its lantern-lit halls, share stories beneath the moonlight, and find peace among kindred spirits. For generations, the palace has served as a gathering place where friendships are forged, melodies echo through jade courtyards, and weary travelers may set aside their burdens for a time.

As the palace continues to welcome guests from every corner of the realm, we seek devoted individuals who wish to contribute to its harmony. Whether as a keeper of ancient records, a steward of festivals, a guardian of the gardens, or a host to wandering visitors, each role plays a part in preserving the warmth and tranquility of the Celestial Lotus Palace. If you desire not merely a position, but a place within a timeless community, we invite you to explore the opportunities that await beyond our gates.
